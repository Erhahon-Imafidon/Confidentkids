Kelly Okolie

Eleven-year-old Kelly Okolie was born with a creative imagination, inquisitive and loves to play football. His eight-year old-sister, Zite Okolie is outspoken, loves food, and is an amazing athlete; she is a 100 meters race runner in her primary school. Kelly and Zite are born Dave and Evelyn Okolie an Igbo, middle-class family. The Okolie's place high value on their christian faith and education.

Dave and his family relocated from onitsha to Abuja about nine months ago. He works as a sales manager with a pharmaceutical company and was transfered to Abuja to set up a new branch office. Their mother, Evelyn owned a fashion designing business where she made school uniforms for school children in Onitsha.
